Ordered
    It prompts your end-users to perform the following actions to connect their financial accounts:
  1. Provide consent to share their financial data.
    1. Select their financial institution.
    2. Enter their username and password to log in with their financial institution.
      1. Answer a multi-factor authentication (MFA) question if their financial institution requires them to.
      2. Select the account they want to share.
      3. If the login is successful, Flinks provides you with a loginId.
  2. Send the loginId to the server that's hosting your website/app.
  3. Your server passes the loginId to Flinks servers and we exchange it for a requestId.
  4. The requestId allows you to start securely receiving the customer's data.
